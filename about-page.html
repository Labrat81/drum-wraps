About

A Little More Information

At Labrat Drum Tech, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable drum wraps that bring a new level of style and customization to your drum kit. Whether you're looking to refresh the look of your drums or create a completely unique aesthetic, we have you covered. Our extensive range of stock designs and finishes caters to a variety of tastes, from classic looks to bold, modern patterns. For those seeking something truly one-of-a-kind, our free custom design service lets you bring your vision to life. Every wrap is crafted from a durable, rigid plastic material with a stunning high-gloss finish, ensuring both visual appeal and lasting quality. Precision-engineered for a perfect drum fit, our wraps are easy to install and built to withstand the demands of regular use. Labrat Drum Tech is here to help you make your drums as unique and expressive as your playing.
